About Hack MoVal

Hack Moval is a Hackathon hosted by Moreno Valley College. Create a project from scratch in our short format 8 hour long hackathon. Choose from one of the two tracks to start your project. This event is open to anyone over the age of 14 years.

If you are interested or want more information about the event, check out the Eventbrite register button at the bottom of the page.
